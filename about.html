Our Mission

UmojaSafe, meaning "Safe Unity" in Swahili, is dedicated to dismantling the structures of online violence targeting African women. We believe digital defense should be collaborative, immediate, and community-led.

How We Work

Real-Time Flares: Victims can instantly broadcast an SOS, sharing the exact link to the harassment.

Ally Action: Verified allies receive these flares in real-time and commit to reporting the content, overwhelming the platform's moderation system for fast takedowns.

Digital Literacy: We provide immediate, actionable safety tips to victims while the community steps in.
